International Foundation Programme for Computer Science

Hey {{ employee.name }}! Are you interested in learning more about computer science?

Study 100% online with the University of London and secure your future career in computer science, regardless of your academic or professional experience and qualifications.

 

The International Foundation Programme for Computer Science is designed to help you gain the foundational mathematics and programming knowledge needed for the University of London Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science.

Why choose this programme?

  • In less than a year, open the door to a world-class degree programme by successfully completing the IFP for Computer Science and being offered a place on the University of London’s online BSc Computer Science degree.
  • Gain valuable hands-on programming experience, using Python to gain an understanding of loops, variables, data types and functions and learn to apply statistical analysis tools like causal models and inference methods to solve real-world problems.
  • Access education with flexible pay by module tuition, where you’ll only pay for the module you register for and if you need to pause your studies, you can do so without any additional cost.
